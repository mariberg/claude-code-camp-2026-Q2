## Newbie Zone (North of Midgaard)

### Discovered Locations
- **Entrance to Newbie Zone** — East of Great Field, message: "Ahhh... the entrance to the newbie zone! Just the place you've been looking for."
- **Beginning of Passage** — First room in zone; newbie monster here; exits n/s (passage leads east)
- **Dirty Hallway** — Has a door to the south (closed); little pet dragon loose here; exits e/w/s
- **A Nexus** — Intersection; n/e exits closed (doors?), s/w open; darker hallway goes south
- **More of The Hallway** — Creepy crawlers here; newbie monster; exits n/s/w
- **Another Corner** — Door to east (closed); exits n/w

### Monsters Spotted
- **Creepy crawlers** — Small scuttling creatures; engaged in combat (punch hits, kicks miss often)
- **Newbie monsters** — Generic enemies; multiple encountered
- **Little pet dragon** — Loose in Dirty Hallway

### Route to Newbie Zone from Midgaard
1. From Temple Altar: north (steps out back of temple toward countryside)
2. Behind Temple Altar (dirt path)
3. Great Field of Midgaard (wide, open field; goes north to pitch-black cave)
4. At certain point in field: east to **Entrance to Newbie Zone**
5. North from entrance: **Beginning of Passage** (dungeon begins)
